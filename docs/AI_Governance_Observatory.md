# AI Governance Observatory

## Overview
The AI Governance Observatory is a geopolitical, interactive dashboard integrated into the Centinela platform. It serves as a centralized intelligence monitor for innovation, regulatory frameworks, and geopolitical events concerning Artificial Intelligence in Latin America. 

## Features
1. **Interactive Cartography (Leaflet Integration)**:
   - Dynamic slippy map providing a dark-themed, geospatial visualization of the region.
   - Provides true coordinates for accurate country placement.

2. **Policy Nodes (9 Active Nations)**:
   - Countries covered: Brasil, Chile, Argentina, Colombia, Perú, México, Uruguay, Costa Rica, and Ecuador.
   - Interactive pins displaying framework maturity (Avanzado, Intermedio, Inicial) through color coding (Emerald, Amber, Rose).
   - Clicking a node expands a comprehensive audit modal displaying:
     - **Ecosystem Overview**: Summarized narrative of the country's AI landscape.
     - **Market Data**: Metrics on adoption rates, total investments, and key national institutions.
     - **Legal Dimensions**: Detailed tracking of Data Privacy, Algorithmic Transparency, Liability, Generative AI policies, and Governing Bodies.
     - **Historical Changelog**: Chronological registry of AI milestones and legislative events.

3. **Regional AI News Terminal**:
   - Aggregated intelligence feed covering ethics, geopolitics, innovation, and markets.
   - **Geographic News Markers**: News events are geolocated on the map as pulsing cyan radio markers.
   - Hover/Click triggers tactical popups summarizing the source, tags, and headlines directly on the map.

## Technologies Used
- Next.js (React)
- Tailwind CSS
- Leaflet & React-Leaflet
- Custom SVG animated markers
- Shadcn UI Components

## Objective
To provide policymakers, security analysts, and researchers an accessible, highly professional, and continuously updated reference point to understand the fragmented landscape of AI regulation and adoption across the continent.
